Our Name

  • Hawaiian: Sea, Ocean

  • Māori: Food, Nourishment

  • Chinese: Change, Transformation

"Kai" is a word rich with meaning across cultures: in Hawaiian, it means sea or ocean; in Māori, food or nourishment; in Chinese, it signifies change or transformation. These meanings perfectly reflect our approach to building. Like a river carving its path, we create homes that respond to their surroundings, flow with intention, and are built to nourish the lives within them.

The name actually has a special backstory. Our company is named after Kai, a much-loved dog who had a serious love for food and bringing people together. That same spirit is what drives us. We believe that a well-built home is more than just walls and a roof; it's where life happens. It's the kitchen where holiday meals are made, the living room where friends gather for game night, the backyard where kids run barefoot in the summer. We create spaces designed for connection, comfort, and the kinds of everyday moments that turn into lasting memories for years to come.

Today

Founded in 2013, Kai has grown steadily by combining meticulous craftsmanship with open, honest communication. While we're known for our award-winning work in Boise's historic neighborhoods, our passion and expertise extend to exceptional new homes across the Treasure Valley. As a full-service, fully licensed and insured general contractor, we specialize in both new construction and remodels. We're a small, tight-knit team that treats every project like our own home, bringing veteran expertise and genuine care to every build.

Our Community

We're proud to call Boise home, bringing our passion for the outdoors, community, and craftsmanship into every home we build or remodel. Whether restoring a historic gem or creating a modern custom home, Kai is your partner in turning ideas into reality.
