Our Build Process

1

Initial Consultation

We meet onsite to discuss your goals, budget, and timeline, then establish a preliminary scope and estimate.

2

Design & Planning

Work with an architect or designer, coordinate with the city for permits, create concept drawings and floor plans, and select finishes and materials.

3

Pre-Construction

Finalize designs and documents, secure permits and approvals, lock in trade partners and suppliers, and complete a detailed budget and schedule.

4

Construction

We build your home according to your plans and selections, maintaining clear communication and quality control every step of the way.

5

Completion & Handover

Final inspections, certificate of occupancy, and a thorough walk-through. We address any warranty items promptly and ensure you're 100% satisfied.
